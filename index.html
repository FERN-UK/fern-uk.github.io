FERN-UK: The Network of French Researchers in the UK

FERN-UK aims to bring together French researchers based in the United Kingdom in order to support Franco-British research collaborations in universities or private sectors, to provide support for exchanges of graduate students and to create a dynamic of information and discussion within the community. This network is extended to British francophile and francophone scientists who wish to participate.
